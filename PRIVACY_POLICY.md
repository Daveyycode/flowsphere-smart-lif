# FlowSphere Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 29, 2025
**Effective Date:** November 29, 2025

## Introduction

FlowSphere ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services (collectively, the "Service").

**Privacy First:** FlowSphere is designed with privacy at its core. Your data is encrypted, stored locally when possible, and never sold to third parties.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Name and email address
- Password (encrypted and never stored in plain text)
- Profile picture (optional)
- Payment information (processed securely by third-party providers - Stripe, Apple Pay, Google Pay)

### 1.2 Device Data
To provide smart home functionality, we collect:
- Device types, names, and room assignments
- Device status (on/off, temperature, brightness, etc.)
- Device usage patterns and automation triggers
- Energy consumption data

### 1.3 Location Data
With your explicit consent, we collect:
- Real-time GPS location of family members who opt-in
- Location history for safety zone alerts
- Home address for weather and automation features
- Traffic and route information

**You can disable location tracking at any time in Settings.**

### 1.4 CCTV and Camera Data
From connected security cameras:
- Video recordings and snapshots (stored locally or on your chosen cloud service)
- Motion detection events and timestamps
- Camera settings and configurations
- AI-analyzed events (e.g., person detected, package delivered)

**Important:** Video data is encrypted end-to-end and only accessible to authorized family members.

### 1.5 Voice Data
When using AI voice assistant features:
- Voice commands and queries
- Voice recordings (temporarily processed, then deleted)
- Voice preferences and settings

**Voice data is processed through OpenAI's API and is not retained after processing.**

### 1.6 Family Information
For family management features:
- Family member names and photos
- Relationship information
- Emergency contact details
- Shared calendars and schedules

### 1.7 Usage Analytics
To improve the Service:
- App usage patterns and feature interactions
- Performance metrics and error logs
- Device type and operating system
- IP address and browser information

### 1.8 Financial Data
For subscription management:
- Subscription tier and billing history
- Payment method information (tokenized)
- Transaction records

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Storage Locations
- **Local Storage:** Device settings, preferences, and cache are stored locally on your device
- **Cloud Storage:** Account data, shared information, and backups are stored on secure cloud servers (Supabase with AWS infrastructure)
- **Video Storage:** CCTV footage is stored locally or on your chosen cloud provider

### 3.2 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures:
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- TLS/SSL encryption for data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) support
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- SOC 2 Type II compliance (pending)

### 3.3 Data Retention
- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active, plus 30 days after deletion
- **Device Data:** Retained for 90 days
- **Location History:** Retained for 30 days (configurable)
- **CCTV Footage:** Retention based on your settings (7-90 days)
- **Voice Data:** Deleted immediately after processing
- **Analytics Data:** Aggregated and anonymized after 12 months

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data
We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

### 4.2 Sharing Within Your Family
- Data you explicitly share with family members (location, calendar, etc.)
- Shared device controls and automation settings
- Emergency information when emergency mode is activated

### 4.3 Service Providers
We share data with trusted third-party service providers:
- **Supabase:** Backend infrastructure and database
- **OpenAI:** AI assistant and voice processing
- **Stripe:** Payment processing
- **Vercel:** Web hosting and CDN
- **Twilio:** SMS notifications (optional)

All service providers are bound by data protection agreements.

### 4.4 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law:
- To comply with legal process or court orders
- To protect our rights, property, or safety
- To prevent fraud or illegal activity
- In emergency situations to protect life

### 4.5 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you via email and in-app notice.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Portability
- **View Your Data:** Access all your data in Settings > Privacy > Download My Data
- **Export Data:** Download a complete copy of your data in JSON format

### 5.2 Correction and Update
- **Update Information:** Edit your profile, family information, and settings at any time
- **Correct Errors:** Contact us to correct any inaccurate data

### 5.3 Deletion
- **Delete Account:** Settings > Account > Delete Account
- **Delete Specific Data:** Remove individual devices, family members, or recordings
- **Right to be Forgotten:** We will delete all your data within 30 days of account deletion

### 5.4 Consent Management
- **Location Tracking:** Enable/disable in Settings > Privacy > Location
- **Camera Access:** Manage in device settings
- **Notifications:** Configure in Settings > Notifications
- **Voice Assistant:** Enable/disable in Settings > AI Voice
- **Marketing:** Opt-out in Settings > Communications

### 5.5 Do Not Track
We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals and will not track users who enable DNT.

## 6. Children's Privacy

FlowSphere is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at privacy@flowsphere.com.

**For users aged 13-18:** Parental consent is required. The Family plan includes parental controls.
