# FlowSphere - Complete Life Management Platform

"One app for your life rhythm" - An AI-driven, privacy-first companion that synchronizes work, family, rest, and home into a single daily flow.

## üöÄ Overview

FlowSphere is a comprehensive life management platform inspired by the elegance of Notion, intelligence of Waze, and engagement of Duolingo. It connects four key life layers into one unified experience with authentication, subscription management, and AI assistance.

## üéØ Architecture Layers

### 1. Personal Layer
- Sleep tracking and DND with emergency override (3 calls in 10 mins)
- AI-powered notification management (email subjects, SMS, calls, messengers)
- Meeting reminders, school activities, exam tracking
- Weekly grocery checker
- Prayer & Bible reading with voice synthesis (2-5 minute sessions)

### 2. Professional Layer
- Email & task summarizer (AI reads subjects and previews only)
- Meeting optimizer with productivity insights
- Remote-accessible timer
- Multi-language meeting transcription (200 languages, auto-detect)
- AI-powered meeting summary on completion

### 3. Family Layer
- Real-time kid tracking with battery status
- Emergency alerts and safety zones
- Household status overview
- Weekend recommendations (new places, restaurants, activities)
- Thursday/Friday updates for family planning

### 4. Home Layer
- CCTV access with live feeds and recording
- IoT device control (lights, thermostats, locks, cameras)
- Smart timers and appliance scheduling
- Energy monitoring and optimization

### AI Assistant Layer
- Voice & text summaries connecting all four layers
- Conversational interface for queries and commands
- Proactive insights and recommendations
- Integration across all life management features
